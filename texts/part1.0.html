1.   How Chinese Culture Goes Global

As an ancient country boasting a history of over five thousand years, China has generated a unique civilization in the east part of Asia. Chinese culture is distinct from any other culture in the world, which has triggered multiple foreigner’s interest to gain a bit deeper insight into this special culture. Thus, Chinese culture demonstrates a strong trend to attain more and more international attention. In this section, we will introduce how Chinese culture goes global through four examples, ranging from Chinese Food and Martial Art to Language Spreading and Computer Game.
